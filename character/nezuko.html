NEZUKO KAMADO

Nezuko is one of the youngest characters in Demon Slayer. She is only 12 years old at the start of the story when she turns into a demon. A few episodes later, she turns 14 years old. But, physically, she didn't grow much after that tragic day. That's something that won't change until Tanjiro finds the antidote.

Her transformation into a demon naturally changed Nezuko's personality. She became braver and more outspoken than when she was still human. However, a number of her good qualities still persist. Despite being a demon, she retains her protective instincts and love for her family.
