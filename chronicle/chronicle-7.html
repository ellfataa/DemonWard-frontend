DEMON SLAYER: KIMETSU NO YAIBA HASHIRA TRAINING ARC

Next there is an arc that focuses on the recovery and training of every demon hunter in the Demon Slayer story. The Hashira Training arc shows hard training for lower level demon hunters to train with the strongest hashiras in Kimetsu No Yaiba with their respective abilities.

The next arc after the sword forging district, is a Hashira training meant to increase the overall health and abilities of the demon slayer. Here you'll see Tengen teaching stamina training, Mitsuri training flexibility, and Muichiro helping the fighters improve their overall speed. Obanai gives teaching tips on becoming an overall better swordsman, Sanemi trains on strength, and Gyomei helps demon slayers build their muscles. This arc is quite brutal but entertaining training like the calm before the storm.
