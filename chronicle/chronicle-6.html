DEMON SLAYER: KIMETSU NO YAIBA SWORDSMITH VILLAGE ARC

Swordsmith Village is the main arc in the currently airing anime Kimetsu No Yaiba season 3. In this arc, we see Tanjiro going to the village of sword smiths to repair his sword. All peace changed after he met two high-level demons.

This arc takes Tanjiro who goes to the village of nichirin sword makers to repair his broken sword. In this arc you can also find Hashira Muichiro and Genya who are one of the selection participants who passed with Tanjiro. However, in this arc you will meet two high ranking demons at once. In this arc, which will be broadcast in 2023, you will see Tanjiro, Nezuko, Genya, and Mitsuri who have to face Hantengu, a High Level Demon who can produce emotional clones. Meanwhile the mist hashira Muichiro has to fight the Upper Level Demon Gyokko. In this arc you will see how Nezuko grows strong enough to survive in the sun.
