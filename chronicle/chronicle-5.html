DEMON SLAYER: KIMETSU NO YAIBA ENTERTAINMENT DISTRICT ARC

Tanjiro and his friends accompany the Hashira Tengen Uzui to an entertainment district where Tengen's female ninja agents were gathering information on a demon before they suddenly disappeared. In order to investigate, Tanjiro and the others disguise themselves as women to sneak in! As they close in on their target, the demon reaches out for the courtesans of the district

This time the Main Trio of this series gets a mission to go to the Red Light District where they have to go undercover in a brothel to find the devil who is wreaking havoc in the city. In this arc you are introduced to Sound Hashira Tengen Uzui and his three wives. This arc has some of the best fights in the series and introduces the Upper Demons Daki and Gyutaro. In this arc you can see the changes in Nezuko who transforms into a strange and stronger person so she can fight Daki, even though she almost loses her consciousness as a human.
